Finding Nemo

A clown fish, is overly cautious with his son, Nemo who has a foreshortened fin. When Nemo swims too close to the surface to prove himself, he is caught by a driver, and horrified Marlin must set out to find him. A blue reef fish named Dory joins Marlin and complicates the encounters with sharks,jellyfish,and ahost of ocean dangers. Meanwhile, Nemo plots his excape from a dentist's fish tank.
